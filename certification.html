Certification

The are a number of components in the Front-end Web Developer programme, namely Web Authoring, Web Development, Interactive Scripting, Project Management and Work Experience, that need to be completed for Quality and Qualifications Ireland (QQI) certification. Each of these components is a Level 6 Minor Award.

Title Code NFQ Level Award Class Award Body
Web Authoring 6N2532 6 Minor QQI
Web Development 6N1277 6 Minor QQI
Interactive Scripting 6N20494 6 Minor QQI
Project Management 6N4090 6 Minor QQI
Work Experience 6N1946 6 Minor QQI

Learners will gain experience in project management, working to deadlines, contribute to projects, along with creating their own online portfolio.

The learner will apply their skills, knowledge, and competence in a live workplace environment during a 12-week work placement provides you the opportunity to apply the knowledge and skills gained in a real work setting. Placements may be arranged by tutor or the learner.

All of the above components are combined to meet the requirements of a Major Award in Digital Media Production.
