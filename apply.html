How to apply

Entry Requirements

  • Education: Leaving Certificate Standard or equivalent or progression from a relevant related course
  • Aptitude: Learners should express a keen desire to become a Front End Web Developer and be fully aware of what is expected in this job role as learners will be required to participate in an Industry placement to gain On-The-Job Experience. Capable of working on own initiative and as part of a team. Problem solving abilities, a flair for troubleshooting and attention to detail. Participation on this course requires consistently meeting the deadlines set for you and completing both individual and group projects that are closely aligned with industry
  • Previous Experience: File Management and Word Processing skills (Essential). Familiarity with Web Terminology and Internet Navigation (Essential). Learners should be able to communicate fluently and professionally in both written and spoken English

Register Today!

You can register your interest in the course by hitting the “Apply Now” button below. This link will take you to fetchcourses.ie where you can register and you will automatically be placed on the waiting list. Alternatively, you can visit your local Intreo office and register your interest there.

Once you have registered your interest, you will be contacted by the course tutor and invited to Finglas Training Centre for an introductory chat about the course. There will then be a computer based test, followed by an interview.

You can also ring Finglas Training Centre and speak directly with the tutor if you have any questions about the course.

There is a maximum student intake of 20 students. The next Front End Web Developer cohort will commence in September 2020. So, apply early to avoid disappointment.
